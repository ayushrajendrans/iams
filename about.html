IAMS

Institute of Advanced Maritime studies is a Skill training institute affiliated by Govt.of india ,Youth Employibility program under ministry of MSME run by National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC). At Institute of Advanced Maritime studies ,we are driven by the belief that the foundation of tomorrow 's workforce lies in the skill developer. Established to develop the skill among youth and provide a great employment opportunities to youth in port across all of the world,shipping and logistics field especially in upcoming largest transhipment port Vizhinjam International Seaport Limited.VISIL also develop a great entrepreneurial opportunities to small scale and large scale firms which need a huge amount skilled employees.We are dedicated to offering advanced skill development and training programs that equip individuals with the practical expertise and knowledge required to excel in their choosen fields
